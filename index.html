What does KWAAI mean?

KW-eye not k-way

We say it’s KW-eye. KWAAI is South African slang for cool, excellent, seriously good — with a little attitude in the cap. Basically: cold coffee that walks in and makes boring leave quietly.

OriginSouth African word energy: cool, bold and very much not shy.
